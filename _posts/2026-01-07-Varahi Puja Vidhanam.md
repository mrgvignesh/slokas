# Sri Varahi Devi Laghu Puja Vidhanam

## 1. Guru DhyAnam
*(Recite Two Times)*

> guru brahmA gurur vishNuhu guru dEvO mahESvaraha 
> guru sAkshAt para brahmA tasmai SrI guravE namaha

---

## 2. GaNapati DhyAnam
SuklAmbaradharam vishNum SaSi varNam chaturbhujam  
prasanna vadanam dhyAyEt sarva vighnOpa SAntayE  
--   
agajAnana padmArkam gajAnanam aharniSam  
anEkadantam bhaktAnAm Ekadantam upAsmahE  

---

## 4. Achamanam
Take a spoon of water in your right hand three times and drink each time:
1. **aim Atma tatvAya svAhA**
2. **klIm vidyA tatvAya svAhA**
3. **sauh Siva tatvAya svAhA**

Release water from between the middle and ring fingers of the right hand into a plate:
> aim klIm sauh sarva tatvEbhyah svAha

---

## 5. PrANAyAmam
Do 3 cycles of breathing by reciting the **gAyatrI mantra** mentally:
* **Breathe in (pUrakam):** 1 time
* **Hold (kumbhakam):** 2 times
* **Release (rEchakam):** 1 time
* **Hold (bahir kumbhakam):** 1 time

---

## 6. Samkalpam
Take **akshatAn** in the right hand, recite the following, then place them on the idol/photo/yantram:

> mama upAtta samasta duritakshaya dvArA SrI vArAhI dEvi prItyartham 
> mama sahakuTumbasya, SrI vArAhI dEvi sampUrNa anugraha siddhyartham 
> yathA Sakti SrI vArAhI dEvi laghu pUjAm karishyE

---

## 7. MUla Mantram
If you have **not** taken Mantra Deeksha from Guruji, use the following:
> **aim glaum vArAhI dEvatAyai namaha**

---

## 10. DhUpam, DIpam & NaivEdyam

### DhUpam (Incense)
mUla mantram + SrI vArAhI para dEvatAyai namaha dhUpam AghrApayAmi

### DIpam (Lamp)
mUla mantram + SrI vArAhI para dEvatAyai namaha dIpam darSayAmi
> lakshmIm kshIra samudra rAja tanayAm SrI ranga dhAmESvarIm  
> dAsI bhUta samasta dEva vanitAm lOkaika dIpAnkurAm  
> SrI man manda kaTAksha labda vibhavAm brahmEndra gangAdharAm tvAm  
> trailOkya kuTumbinIm sarisijAm vandE mukunda priyAm

### NaivEdyam (Offering)
*Offer Red Fruits or Sweet Pongal.* Sprinkle water in clockwise direction around the plate:
1. Om bhUr bhuvassuvaha tat savitur varENyam bharghO dEvasya dhImahi dhiyO yOnah prachOdayAt
2. Om ApO jyotI rasOmruthm brahma bhUrbhuvaha suvarOm
3. **Daytime:** satyam tvarthEna parishinchAmi 
4. **Nighttime:** Rtam tvA satyEna parishinchAmi
5. amruta mastu
6. amrutO pastaraNamasi

**Offer to the Goddess:**
* Om praNAya svAhA, Om apAnAya svAhA
* Om vyAnAya svAhA, Om udAnAya svAhA
* Om samAnAya svAhA, Om brahmanE svAhA

Offer water: *madhyE madhyE amruta pAniyam samarpayAmi*

**Final Cleaning:**
* amruthApidhAnamasi uttarA oupOSanam samarpayAmi
* hastau prakshAlayAmi pAdau prakshAlayAmi
* sudda AchamanIyam samarpayAmi
* SrI vArAhI dEvi prIthyartham madhura naivEdyam nivEdayAmi SrI paramEsvarI arpaNamastu.

## 12. Conclusion
Take akshatAn in right hand, pour water on them, and drop in a plate:

> mantra hInam, kriyA hInam, bhakti hInam, SraddhA hInam dravya hInam 
> paramEshvari yat pUjitam mayAdEvi paripUrNam tadasthutE
> mayAkruta Etat pUjA sarvam SrI vArAhI dEvatArpaNamastu

**Final Prayer:**
Om pUrNamadah pUrNamidam pUrNAt pUrNamudachyatE  
pUrNasya pUrNamAdAya pUrNamEvA avaSishyatE  
Om SAntihi, SAntihi, SAntihi.
