# Sri Hanumad Badabanala Stotram

The **Sri Hanumad Badabanala Stotram** (also known as the *Hanuman Vadavanala Stotra*) is a powerful hymn dedicated to Lord Hanuman. It was composed by Vibhishana (the brother of Ravana) to invoke the protective, fierce, and healing energies of Hanuman to eliminate negative energies, diseases, enemies, and fears. 

---

## Introduction & Dhyanam (Meditation)

**Asya Sri Hanumad Badabanala Stotra Mantrasya,** **Vibhishana Rishihi, Sri Badabanala Hanuman Devata,** **Hram Bījam, Hrim Shaktihi, Hroom Kilakam,** **Sri Hanumad Badabanala Stotra Paripathana Jape Viniyogah.** *(Meaning: For this mantra of the Badabanala Hanuman Stotra, the Rishi is Vibhishana, the deity invoked is Badabanala Hanuman, Hram is the seed, Hrim is the power, Hroom is the pillar, and it is chanted to secure the complete grace of Hanuman.)*

### Dhyana Shlokam
**Manojavam Maruta Tulya Vegam,** **Jitendriyam Buddhimatam Varishtham.** **Vatatmajam Vanara Yootha Mukhyam,** **Shri Rama Dutam Sharanam Prapadye.** *(Meaning: I take refuge in the messenger of Sri Rama, who is swift as the mind, fast as the wind, master of the senses, most excellent among the wise, son of the Wind God, and chief of the vanara army.)*

---

## Stotram Lyrics (English Transliteration)

**Om Namo Bhagavate Sri Parama Purushaya Sadashivaya,** **Om Namo Bhagavate Hanumate Badabanala Analaya,** **Sringara Veera Adbhuta Raudra Beebhatsa Hasya,** **Bhayanaka Karuna Rasa Paripuritaya.** **Om Parama Shiva Avataraya, Shankara Suvānaya,** **Anjana Garbha Sambhutaya, Sri Rama Charana Kamala,** **Aradhakaya, Bhakta Jana Manah Kalpataruve.** **Aum Rakshoraja Vibhishana Prana Rakshakaya,** **Sita Shoka Vinashanaya, Lankadahanaya,** **Yama Pasha Kala Pasha Yama Danda,** **Kala Bhairava Bhaya Nivaranaya.** **Om Jvara Jalam, Atapa Jalam, Bhuta Jalam,** **Preta Jalam, Pishacha Jalam, Brahma Rakshasa Jalam,** **Yaksha Rakshasa Jalam, Shakini Dhakini Jalam,** **Choragni Bhaya Jalam, Mantra Tantra Jalam,** **Abhichara Jalam, Chhinna Chhinna Karaya.** **Om Sarva Vyadhi, Ghraha Ucchatanaya,** **Om Hram Hrim Hroom Hraim Hraum Hrah,** **Om Sarva Dushta Graha Nivaranaya,** **Om Namo Bhagavate Anjaneyaya Amrita Shravanaya.** **Ayur Arogya Aishvarya,** **Putra Pautra Praghi Vardhanaya,** **Mata Purusha Vashakaranaya,** **Om Hram Hrim Hroom Hraim Hraum Hrah Om Phat Swaha.** **Rajadwāre Bhaya Nivari,** **Shatru Mukha Stambhani,** **Shatru Sainya Vinashani,** **Sarva Karya Sadhani.** **Om Chhinnam Chhinnam, Bhinnam Bhinnam,** **Kham Kham Kham, Khas Khas Khas,** **Daha Daha, Pacha Pacha,** **Māraya Māraya, Vinashaya Vinashaya,** **Shatru Senam Vidravaya Vidravaya.** **Om Hram Hrim Hroom Hanumate,** **Badabanala Analaya Namaha,** **Om Phat Swaha,** **Harihi Om Tatsat Sri Krishna Arpanamastu.** ---

## Key Benefits of Chanting
* **Protection:** Wards off negative energies, evil eyes, black magic, and planetary afflictions (Graha Dosha).
* **Healing:** Relieves chronic illnesses, fevers (Jvara), and physical ailments.
* **Removal of Fear:** Eradicates the fear of death, enemies, authority figures, and unknown anxieties.
* **Success:** Clears obstacles and ensures success (*Sarva Karya Sadhani*) in challenging endeavors.
