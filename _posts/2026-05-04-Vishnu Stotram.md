# Shri Vishnu Shodasha Nama Stotram
## (The 16 Names of Lord Vishnu)

A guide for invoking the different forms of Lord Vishnu during specific activities of life.

---

### 1. Daily Essentials
**Verse 1**
*auṣadhe cintayed viṣṇuṃ bhojane ca janārdanam |* *śayane padmanābhaṃ ca vivāhe ca prajāpatim || 1 ||*

| Situation | Name to Remember | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Taking Medicine** | **Viṣṇu** | The All-Pervading Healer |
| **Eating Food** | **Janārdana** | The Provider of Mankind |
| **Going to Sleep** | **Padmanābha** | He with the Lotus Navel |
| **During Marriage** | **Prajāpati** | The Lord of Creation |

---

### 2. Conflict & Travel
**Verse 2**
*yuddhe cakradharaṃ devaṃ pravāse ca trivikramam |* *nārāyaṇaṃ tanutyāge śrīdharaṃ priyasaṅgame || 2 ||*

| Situation | Name to Remember | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **During Conflict/War** | **Cakradhara** | The Wielder of the Discus |
| **While Traveling** | **Trivikrama** | The Conqueror of the Three Worlds |
| **At the Time of Death** | **Nārāyaṇa** | The Ultimate Refuge |
| **Meeting Loved Ones** | **Śrīdhara** | The Possessor of Fortune |

---

### 3. Fear & Danger
**Verse 3**
*duḥsvapne smara govindaṃ saṅkaṭe madhusūdanam |* *kānane nārasiṃhaṃ ca pāvake jalaśāyinam || 3 ||*

| Situation | Name to Remember | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **After a Bad Dream** | **Govinda** | The Protector of Senses/Cows |
| **In Times of Distress** | **Madhusūdana** | The Destroyer of Demons |
| **In the Forest/Wild** | **Nārasiṃha** | The Lion-Man Protector |
| **In the Midst of Fire** | **Jalaśāyina** | He who rests on the Waters |

---

### 4. Environments & Success
**Verse 4**
*jalamadhye varāhaṃ ca parvate raghunandanam |* *gamane vāmanaṃ caiva sarvakāryeṣu mādhavam || 4 ||*

| Situation | Name to Remember | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **In the Water** | **Varāha** | The Divine Boar (Rescuer of Earth) |
| **On a Mountain** | **Raghunandana** | Lord Rama (Joy of the Raghus) |
| **While Walking** | **Vāmana** | The Divine Dwarf Incarnation |
| **In All Endeavors** | **Mādhava** | The Lord of Knowledge & Lakshmi |

---

### 5. Conclusion (The Benefit)
**Verse 5**
*ṣoḍaśaitāni nāmāni prātarutthāya yaḥ paṭhet |* *sarvapāpavinirmukto viṣṇuloke mahīyate || 5 ||*

> **Translation:** One who recites these sixteen names every morning upon waking becomes free from all sins and attains the supreme abode of Lord Vishnu.
