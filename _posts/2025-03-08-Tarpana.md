# Tarpana for Devas, Rishis, and Pitris

## 1. Sankalpam (The Vow)
*Hold a small amount of water in your right palm and recite:*

> om adya śubha dine śubha muhūrte devarṣipitṛprītyarthaṃ devarṣipitṛtarpaṇam yathāśakti kariṣye |

---

## 2. Deva Tarpana
**Direction:** Face **East**.  
**Posture:** Normal sitting position.  
**Method:** Release water from the **tips of the fingers** (Deva Tirtha). Use water containing Akshatas (sanctified rice).

* om brahmā tṛpyatām |
* om viṣṇuḥ tṛpyatām |
* om rudraḥ tṛpyatām |
* om prajāpatiḥ tṛpyatām |
* om devāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om devyaḥ tṛpyantām |
* om vasavaḥ tṛpyantām |
* om rudrāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om ādityāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om vedāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om rṣayaḥ tṛpyantām |
* om gandharvāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om apsarasaḥ tṛpyantām |
* om nāgāḥ tṛpyantām |
* om oṣadhayaḥ tṛpyantām |

## 3. Rishi Tarpana
**Direction:** Face **East**.  
**Posture:** Sit with the **right knee in contact with the earth**.  
**Method:** Release water from the **base of the small finger** or tips. Use water with Akshatas.

* om kaśyapaḥ tṛpyatām |
* om atriḥ tṛpyatām |
* om vasiṣṭhaḥ tṛpyatām |
* om viśvāmitraḥ tṛpyatām |
* om gautamaḥ tṛpyatām |
* om bharadvājaḥ tṛpyatām |
* om jamadagniḥ tṛpyatām |
* om aṅgirāḥ tṛpyatām |
* om kutsaḥ tṛpyatām |
* om bhṛguḥ tṛpyatām |

## 4. Pitri Tarpana (Ancestral Offerings)

### Important Transitions:
* **Direction:** Face **South**.
* **Posture:** Sit with the **left leg on the ground and the right leg on top of the left**.
* **Ingredients:** Add **black sesame seeds** (Tila) to the water.
* **Method (Pitri Tirtha):** Release water sideways from the area **between the thumb and index finger**.
* **Darbha:** If using 3 darbhas, place them on the index finger aligned with the tip. Water should touch the base of the darbhas as it leaves the palm.



### General Ancestors (3 Offerings Each)
*Release water 3 times for each of the following:*

**To beings who played the role of Father (Pitri):**
> mama sarvajanmasu pitaraḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || (1) (2) (3) ||

**To beings who played the role of Grandfather (Pitamaha):**
> mama sarvajanmasu pitāmahāḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || (1) (2) (3) ||

**To beings who played the role of Great Grandfather (Prapitamaha):**
> mama sarvajanmasu prapitämahäù tåpyantäm idaà tilodakaà svadhäyibhyaù tebhyaù svadhä namaù || (1) (2) (3) ||

**To beings who played the role of Mother (Matri):**
> mama sarvajanmasu mātaraḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tābhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || (1) (2) (3) ||

**To beings who played the role of Paternal Grandmother (Pitamahi):**
> mama sarvajanmasu pitāmahyaḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tābhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || (1) (2) (3) ||

**To beings who played the role of Paternal Great Grandmother (Prapitamahi):**
> mama sarvajanmasu prapitāmahyaḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tābhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || (1) (2) (3) ||

### Specific Relations (1 Offering Each)
*Release water 1 time for each:*

* **Wife:** mama sarvajanmasu patnyaḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tābhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||
* **Husband:** mama sarvajanmasu patayaḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||
* **Son:** mama sarvajanmasu putrāḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||
* **Daughter:** mama sarvajanmasu kanyāḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tābhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||
* **Dear Ones:** mama sarvajanmasu āptajanāḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||
* **Lineage (Kula):** mama sarvajanmasu svakulajātāḥ tṛpyantām idaṃ tilodakaṃ svadhāyibhyaḥ tebhyaḥ svadhā namaḥ || 1 ||

## 5. Conclusion
**Method:** Throw away remaining water. Adjust sacred thread to normal position (Upavita). Face **East**.

> bhagavān pitṛsvarūpī janārdana vāsudevaḥ prīyatām | 
> om sarvaṃ śrī kṛṣṇārpaṇamastu | 
> om śāntiḥ śāntiḥ śāntiḥ |
