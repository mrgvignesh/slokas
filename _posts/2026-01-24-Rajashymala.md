## Introduction
These sixteen names (Shodasa Namas) are dedicated to **Sri Raja Shyamala Devi**, the Ministerial power of Goddess Lalita Tripurasundari. Reciting these names is said to bestow eloquence, knowledge, and administrative success.

## Benefits and Usage
* **Auspicious Times:** Particularly potent when recited during *Shyamala Navaratri* or on Fridays.
* **Bija Mantras:** For ritualistic worship, the names are often prefixed with the Bija mantras: `$aiṁ klīṁ sauḥ$`.
* **Purpose:** Helps in mastering fine arts, music, and enhancing communicative skills.

> **Note:** Proper pronunciation is key to the vibration of these mantras. If possible, listen to a recorded version by a practitioner to ensure the phonetics are correct.
