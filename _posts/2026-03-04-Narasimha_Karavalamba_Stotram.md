# Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Karavalamba Stotram
*Composed by Adi Shankaracharya*

**Note:** Each verse ends with the powerful prayer: *Laksmi-pate kamala-nabha suresa visno, yajnesa yajna-madhu-sudana dehi me karavalambam.* ---

### Verse 1
Srimad-akalanka-paripurna-sasanka-bimba-
vaktrambu-ruha-saranatamara-sayataksa |
nrsimha raudra-sikhi-pavaka-netradharin
laksmipate kamalanabha suresa raksa ||

### Verse 2
Samsara-dava-dahanatmaka-bhikarana-
tagvyala-bhisana-varat-ishara-dhvajasya |
alambanam mama puras-sarabhasya dehi
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 3
Samsara-jala-patitasya jagan-nivasa-
sarvendriyartha-badisagra-jhasopamasya |
protkampita-prathama-vismrta-dharma-pasa-
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 4
Samsara-kupam-ati-ghoram-agadha-mulam-
samprapya duhkha-sata-sarpa-samakulasya |
dinasya deva krpanaya purushottamasya
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 5
Samsara-bhikara-karindra-karabhighata-
nispista-marmavapusah sakalartinasya |
prana-prayana-bhaya-bhita-samakulasya
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 6
Samsara-sarpa-visadigdha-mahogra-tivra-
damstravidagdha-vapusas-cakitayasya |
samsara-roga-bhisaja-paramarthamurtes
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 7
Samsara-vrksam-agha-bijam-ananta-karma-
sakha-yutam-karana-patram-ananga-puspam |
aruhya duhkha-phalam-adanato mahatah
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 8
Samsara-sagara-visala-karala-kala-
nakra-graha-grasanigrahita-vigrahasya |
bhitasya bhima-bhaya-prapiditasya vibho
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 9
Samsara-sagara-nimagna-mamanantaram
dinam vilokayatu vibho karunanidhe |
sarvaparadha-mapanuda mama sarvabhauma
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 10
Samsara-ghora-vipine-carato bhayasya-
vyaghra-dvipa-dvivida-varaha-vancitasya |
asrayama mama dehi suresa vibho
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 11
Baddhva gale yama-bhatah bahu-tarjayantah-
karsanti yatra bhava-pasa-satair-nibaddham |
ekakinam paravasam ca sa-bhitam-imam
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 12
Laksmipate kamalanabha suresa visno
vaikuntha-natha purusottama vasudeva |
devendra-vandya mrdu-pankaja-padu-padma
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 13
Brahmendra-rudra-marudarka-kirita-koti-
sanghattita-sthita-padamburuha-dvayasya |
sarva-paradha-mapanuda mama deva-deva
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 14
Andhasya me hrta-viveka-maha-dhanasya
corair-mahotsava-balair-indriya-namadheyaih |
mohandha-kupa-kuhare vinipatitasya
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 15
Prahlada-narada-para-sara-pundarika-
vyasadi-bhagavata-pungava-hrn-nivasa |
bhaktanurakta-paripalana-parijata
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 16
Laksmipate-stotra-midam patanti ye
bhaktya-vibhuti-phala-daiva-mupaiti sighram |
te dhanam-dhanyam-akhilam bhuvi bhukta-bhoga
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||

### Verse 17
Yat-krta-punyam-idam-pathati prabhate
nityam nrsimha-paricarya-prasannacittah |
sarvan-kamana-vaptuya-datulams-tathaisa
laksmipate kamalanabha dehi me karavalambam ||
